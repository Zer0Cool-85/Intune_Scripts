Manual Autopilot Registration
This document is to be used in situations where a new device was not registered with Autopilot at the time it was purchased. The following steps will require an admin account with the Intune Administrator role, and should be completed during the Out of Box Experience (OOBE): 
 
1.	Power on the device and go through the first couple OOBE screens to select the language, keyboard layout, and then stop after you are connected to a network. 

2.	Now, you will open a command prompt by pressing Shift+F10 or FN+Shift+F10 and input the following commands:

          PowerShell.exe -ExecutionPolicy Bypass
          Install-Script -name Get-WindowsAutopilotInfo -Force 
                      *note* NuGet provider is required, type “Y” to continue when prompted.
          Set-ExecutionPolicy -Scope Process -ExecutionPolicy RemoteSigned 
          Get-WindowsAutopilotInfo -Online -GroupTag “GroupTag”

3.	A window asking you to sign in will open, and here you will sign in using your admin account.

4.	After you authenticate, the script will run, and the device will be joined to AzureAD and registered with Autopilot. It can take several minutes to complete. 

5.	Once its finished, type in the following command to reboot the device:
Restart-Computer
6.	When the device boots back to the OOBE, it should now run through the Windows Autopilot provisioning process and after you run through the first couple screens you will get a Company login prompt. At this point you can shut down the device and ship it to the user.

a.	If you do not see this login prompt, wait a few moments, reboot, and try again. It can take a few moments for the Autopilot registration to complete.
